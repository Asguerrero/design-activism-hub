THE RIGHT PIXEL CAN UNLOCK CHANGE

DESIGN ACTIVISM USES the design process to facilitate positive change in the world. Design activists work with communities and interdisciplinary teams to tackle socioeconomic, political, and environmental causes. [1]

“I believe that we are at a global turning point. Design now has to be for social good. We require a new action frame.”

Victor Margolin, 2002

Design Historian

THE 6 PRINCIPLES OF DESIGN ACTIVISM

3 DIFFERENT TYPES OF DESIGN ACTIVISTS

SOLVER

A designer who lends their skills to help a community address a problem. [2]

FORECASTER

A designer who foresees a potential issue and uses design to avoid the creation of the problem. [2]

CAMPAGINER

A designer who helps a community call attention to a problem that is unrecognized by authorities. They assist the community in creating an awareness campaign. [2]
